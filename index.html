About Us

Located in the heart of Kailua town, Custom Built Cabinetry, Inc. has been owned and operated for over 20 years by kama`aina cabinetmaker Ian M. Osborne.

Custom Built Cabinetry specializes in the design, fabrication, and installation of custom kitchens, bathrooms, entertainment centers, any type of cabinet project that you may have in mind.

Whether you have existing plans or are starting from scratch, we are here to assist you in tailoring your project to meet your needs and exceed your expectations.
